## 🎯 BRAND GUIDELINES

### Logo Usage
- **Minimum Size**: 36px width for nav, 44px for auth pages
- **Clear Space**: Maintain 10px padding around logo
- **Colors**: Hexagon in #1D9E75, plus/cross in white
- **Wordmark**: Always use "Care" + "vell" with specified colors

### Color Usage
- **Primary Actions**: #1D9E75 (Teal)
- **Backgrounds Dark**: #085041 (Forest) or #04342C (Deep Forest)
- **Backgrounds Light**: #E1F5EE (Mist)
- **Accents**: #5DCAA5 (Mint) or #9FE1CB (Foam)
- **Emergency**: #E24B4A only for critical actions

### Typography
- **Headings**: Inter 500, #2C2C2A
- **Body**: Inter 400, #2C2C2A
- **Captions**: Inter 400, 12px, #888780
- **Links**: Inter 500, #1D9E75
